Exceptional 5-Star Dining

Bear Trail Dining

Classic 5-Course Gourmet Dinner in our European Style Dining Room or as Room Service brought to you. And Classic Style Breakfast In-Bed.

5 Course Gourmet Meal

1 amuse bouche,
2 Starters (Appy, Soup, or Salad),
1 main Entree Choice,
1 Dessert Choice.

Dinner Reservations

Reserve Table Between 11am - 5pm. At the bar or call 866-202-1179 ext 108. Seating is between 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm (to 9:00 pm Fridays)

Dinner Menu Highlights

Starters

  • Winter Salad: Roasted red and yellow beets, sautéed winter greens, horseradish cashew cream, beet chips.
  • Crispy Pork Belly Strips: Maple miso glaze, spice blend, pickled carrot, dressed arugula, toasted sesame seeds.
  • Charcuterie for Two: House gravlax, venison terrine, pepper salami, cheeses, house pickles, and fig chutney.

Main Entrées

  • Grilled Beef Tenderloin (4 oz): Red wine demi glace, confit potato, brown butter sweet potato purée.
  • Pan Seared Chicken Supreme: Stuffed with Brie cheese and cremini mushrooms, velouté white wine sauce, blueberry coulis.
  • Baked Seafood Linguini: Matane shrimp, bay scallops and white fish, white wine coconut sauce, Mozzarella, and herbed crispy panko.
  • Rigatoni Venison Ragout: Slow braised venison in red wine tomato sauce, cremini mushrooms.

Dessert & Breakfast

Decadent Desserts

  • White Chocolate Cheesecake: Whip cream, seasonal berries, berry coulis.
  • Chocolate Tower: 70% Belgium chocolate, white Belgium chocolate, raspberry gel.
  • Artisan Cheese Plate: Featuring Bluebrie and Aged Caprano, served with berry purée, chutney, and fruit bread crostinis.

Breakfast in Bed

  • Eggs Benedict: Poached eggs, Canadian bacon or smoked salmon & Hollandaise on English Muffin.
  • Belgium Waffle: Topped with Seasonal Compote and Maple Syrup Whip Cream (GF option available).
  • Couples Sunrise: 2 Eggs, Served with Bacon or Ham, Home Fries and Toast.
  • Omelette & Home Fries: Custom-made with choice of 3 ingredients (e.g., Smoked Salmon, Brie, Asparagus).

Wine Information
